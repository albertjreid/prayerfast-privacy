Privacy Policy for PrayerFast Scheduler

Last updated: December 15, 2025

DATA COLLECTION

PrayerFast Scheduler does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data to external servers. All data entered into the app (prayers, fasting schedules, reminders, and any audio recordings) is stored locally on your device only.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Our app does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks that collect user data.

DATA STORAGE

All information you enter into PrayerFast Scheduler remains on your device. We do not have access to your data, and it is not transmitted to or backed up on our servers. All data is encrypted and stored locally using SQLite database.

PERMISSIONS

The app may request the following permissions to provide functionality:

  • Notifications: To send you prayer and fasting reminders at your scheduled times
  • Microphone: To enable optional audio recording features for prayer notes (recordings are stored locally only)

These permissions are used only for app functionality and do not transmit any data externally.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

DATA SECURITY

Your data is encrypted and stored securely on your device. Since we do not transmit or store data on external servers, your information remains private and under your control.

YOUR RIGHTS

You maintain full control over your data. You can delete any or all information stored in the app at any time by uninstalling the application or using the app's delete functions.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
