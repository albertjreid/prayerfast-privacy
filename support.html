Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a fasting schedule?

Tap "Start Your Fast" on the home screen, select your fasting type, set your dates and times, and add optional prayer reminders.

Where is my data stored?

All your data (fasting schedules, prayers, notes) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your information.

How do I delete a fast?

Navigate to your active or completed fasts and swipe left (iOS) or long-press (Android) to delete.

Can I export my fasting history?

This feature is coming in a future update. Currently, data remains on your device only.

The app isn't sending me notifications

Please check your device settings and ensure notifications are enabled for PrayerFast Scheduler.
